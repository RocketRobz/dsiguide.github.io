    HiyaCFW (Flipnotehax)

    If you need help, ask the Nintendo Homebrew Discord!.
    I worked hard on this guide! If you want, you can donate through PayPal or Bitcoin!

    Make sure to follow the guide closely to ensure that you do everything correctly.

    Make sure to download the right files before proceeding with the guide.

    What you need

    • The latest release of Flipnotehax (select from chart below corresponding to your reigon)
    Releases Flipnotehax (USA) Flipnotehax (EUR) Flipnotehax (JPN)
    • An SD Card with at least 128MB of space
    • Flipnote Studio for Nintendo DSi
    • The latest release of RocketLauncher .nds Maker
    • The latest release of HiyaCFW
    • The latest release of FWTool
    • A Nintendo DS Game Card labelled as NTR (see the picture below to know if you have an NTR-labelled Game Card)

    Instructions

    1. Power off your device
    2. Insert your SD card into your computer
    3. Copy the contents of flipnotehax.zip to the root of your SD card
    4. Copy the contents of HiyaCFW.zip to the root of your SD card and rename HiyaCFW.nds to boot.nds
    5. Copy the contents of FWTool.zip to the root of your SD card and rename FWTool to 2222.nds
    6. Rename RocketLauncher.nds to 2222.nds and place the file in the root of your SD
    7. Remove the SD card from your computer and insert it into the SD card slot on your DSi
    8. Insert your NTR-labelled Game Card
    9. Power on your device and open Flipnote Studio
    10. Find the folder named ugopwn and open it
    11. Select the first Flipnote and press Details
      12. , then Edit
    12. "Copy" the first frame
    13. Exit the Flipnote, and proceed to open and edit the second one
    14. On the second Flipnote, in the frame menu, press right on the D-Pad (if right handed) or the A button (if left handed)
    15. Tap the Paste button exactly 122 times
    16. Press the Erase button, followed by the Paste button.
    17. Choose the options to dump nand_dsi.bin and CID.bin
    18. Power off your device
    19. Copy nand_dsi.bin and CID.bin to your PC
    20. Obtain the ROM file of the NTR cart you have. You can also dump it with a 3DS using GodMode9. NOTE: We do not condone piracy.
    21. Open RocketLauncherNDSMaker.exe
    22. Follow the on-screen instructions and put the resulting file(s) on your SD Card
    23. Rename RocketLauncher.nds to 2222.nds and choose replace.
    24. Once again, power on your device and open Flipnote Studio
    25. Find the folder named ugopwn and open it
    26. Select the first Flipnote and press Details
      27. , then Edit
    27. "Copy" the first frame
    28. Exit the Flipnote, and proceed to open and edit the second one
    29. On the second flipnote, in the frame menu, press right on the D-Pad (if right handed) or the A button (if left handed)
    30. Tap the Paste button exactly 122 times
    31. Press the Erase button, followed by the Paste button.
    32. You should now be running HiyaCFW! You must repeat the Flipnote process every time to start HiyaCFW.
