    What is Homebrew?

    Homebrew means software that is not made or authorized by Nintendo. This lets you play games made by homebrew developers, edit your saves, and much, much more.

    DSi Homebrew is 100% Free! As long as you have the required materials, you're good to go.

    What is Custom Firmware?

    Custom Firmware (“CFW”) enables you to use more advanced hacks that any normal DSiWare homebrew can't acheive, like installing DSiWare backups, custom DSiWare apps, and anything else that only ARM7 can do.

    CFW can be easily set up on any console that is on 1.4.5 or lower.

    What does this guide install?

    This guide installs a version of DSi Custom Firmware known as "HiyaCFW."

    What can I do with Custom Firmware?

    • Play all DSiWare games, without reigon locking!
    • Use “ROM hacks” for DSiWare games.
    • Manage saves for many DSiWare games
    • Play games for older systems by using emulators.
    • Install homebrew titles to your system, and have them appear on your HOME Menu
    • Allows the installation of NTRBootHax (coming soon)
    • Run many old Nintendo DS flash carts that were blocked long ago or never worked on Nintendo DSi

    What do I need to know before starting?

    • You should know that there is a chance your console could brick (not function at all) if you do something incorrectly. The chance of this is ALMOST 0% IF you follow instructions.
    • This guide will work on DSi and DSiXL in all regions on firmware 1.4.5 or below. NOTE: THIS WILL NOT WORK ON ORIGINAL DS OR DS LITE!
    • Keep your DSi plugged in AT ALL TIMES DURING THIS PROCESS to avoid sudden power loss!
    • You may need an NTR DS cart for this process to work. To check if your DS Cart is NTR, look at the label.
